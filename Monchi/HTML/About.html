About Monchi Booth

Monchi Booth was created for people who love capturing memories without needing expensive hardware. Just your browser, your smile, and our vibes.

Our Mission

To bring the fun and nostalgia of photo booths to everyone, everywhere, with zero hassle.

Why I Built It

I wanted a simple way to create photo strips for parties and events without needing a physical booth. So I built one that fits in your browser!

How It Works

Monchi Booth uses your device's camera to capture photos, processes them locally, and generates a photo strip you can download instantly. No uploads, no storage, just fun!
